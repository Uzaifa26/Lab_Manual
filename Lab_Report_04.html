Inline CSS Example

Morning is the beginning of a new day, bringing with it freshness, hope, and energy.
The rising sun spreads light across the sky, birds sing melodious songs, and the cool
breeze creates a calm and peaceful atmosphere. Morning is the best time to plan the
day, study, or enjoy a walk in nature. It reminds us that every day is a new chance to
learn, grow, and do better than yesterday.

Internal CSS Example

Nature is the beautiful world around us, full of mountains, rivers, forests, plants, and animals.
It provides us with fresh air to breathe, water to drink, and food to eat. The colors of flowers,
the songs of birds, and the changing seasons make life peaceful and joyful. Nature is also our teacher,
showing us balance and harmony in everything. It is our responsibility to take care of it, so future
generations can enjoy the same beauty and benefits.

External CSS Example

Night is the quiet time of the day when the sun sets, the sky turns dark, and stars begin to shine. It brings rest
and peace after a long day of work. The cool breeze and calm surroundings create a relaxing atmosphere, helping
people to refresh their minds and bodies. Night also gives us time to reflect, dream, and prepare for the
opportunities of a new day.
